The Grace Victim Support Centre was established in Nelspruit, Mpumalanga in 2003, originally to serve as a temporary safe haven for people in difficult and dangerous circumstances to recover from their trauma.

 

CURRENT PROJECTS:

  • Temporary shelter and counselling facility

  • Abused elderly persons shelter

  • Grace adult literacy programme

  • Just a sandwich please!
